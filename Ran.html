Manufacturers of Premium quality precast boundary walls

Randhawa Precast Industries is a name that is considered synonym with the strong values of honesty and professionalism. Although there are many precast companies working with the name of ‘Randhawa’, our company is the founder of precast concrete product manufacturing in Pakistan. Our company was established in 1986 by “Muhammad Anwar Randhawa” with a vision of providing excellent construction and precast concrete technology in Pakistan. We have been in the forefront of bringing about a revolution in the precast industry of Pakistan. Since our conception, we have accumulated a diverse experience of nearly half a century in the manufacturing and development of concrete advanced and innovative precast products.

Our company has firmly established the traditions of superior quality work obtained through profound knowledge and experience combined with total commitment and truthful approach to delivering outstanding construction products and services for a variety of building structures.

As a result of this, our company has emerged as a distinct name in the precast concrete technology in Pakistan. We have made and will continue to make earnest contributions towards enhancing the quality of construction materials and raising the standards of living in our country. We have made valuable contributions towards increasing the quality, safety and the cost efficiency of the construction products.

During our journey, we have always maintained high-quality standards and have strived to offer finest quality products at the pocket-friendly rates which not just help to make your construction safe and strong but also affordable.
